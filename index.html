Robot Car

CA2

Introduction

I will be doing a Robot Car with the use of ardruino and hopefully with the help of ESP8266 to include the IOT inside the car. The transistor will be used as a BJT transistor electronic switch to power the motor of the car. It is to prolong the life time of Arduino by not letting the Adruino power the motor directly. With the help of the Wifi Module(ESP8266), I am proposing to use our mobile phones to control the movements of the car. The use of Ultra Sonic Sensor is the preent the car from moving forward when it detects an obstacle infront of the car.

Components Needed

  • Arduino UNO
  • 2 TIP110 Transistors
  • 2 40mm diameter acrylic wheel
  • Wires
  • 1 Caster Wheel
  • 1 Ultra Sonic Sensor
  • 1 ESP8266(Wifi Module)
  • 2 DC Motor(3V)
  • 1 x2(AAA)Battery Casing
  • Portable Phone Power Bank
